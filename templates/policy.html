Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences (such as login information, language settings, and other display preferences) over a period of time so that you don't have to re-enter them whenever you return to the site.

Types of Cookies We Use

We do not use advertising or performance cookies on our website. The cookies we use are solely for essential and functionality purposes, to enhance your browsing experience.

  1. Functionality Cookies These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. The information these cookies collect may be anonymized, and they cannot track your browsing activity on other websites.

Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use advertising or performance cookies on our website. The cookies we use are solely for essential and functionality purposes, to enhance your browsing experience.

  1. Advertising Cookies We do not use advertising cookies to track users across different websites or display personalized advertisements.

  2. Performance Cookies We do not use performance or analytics cookies to track user behavior or measure website performance.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default, but you can modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies if you prefer. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Go to Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Site Permissions > Cookies and site data.

For more detailed information on how to control cookies, you can visit https://www.allaboutcookies.org/.
